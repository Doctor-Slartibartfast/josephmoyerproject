All About Me

I’m a photographer, writer, and projection mapping artist with a perfectly shaped bald head (or so I’m told) who believes the world is full of small moments worth noticing—and occasionally worth laughing at. Whether I’m capturing a sunrise that made me stop in my tracks, turning a blank wall into a galaxy of moving light, or stringing words together until they become something that makes you pause, I’m here to help you find beauty, humor, and a fresh perspective in the everyday.

If you’re curious about the guy behind the camera, I invite you to keep reading. And if you just want the gist, skip to the bottom for a brief summary.

I’ve been called many things. The “coolest nerd ever” (possibly my mom), “crazy and obnoxious but still lovable,” and even—true story—“an excellent service dog.” That last one came from a guy who said he always smiled when I entered the room. I still don’t know whether to be flattered or concerned.

Whatever I am, I know this: I love a good story.

Everyone has one. Twins raised in the same home will still see the world differently. That’s the magic. And I spend a lot of time trying to capture it—through photography, writing, video, and projection mapping. Whether it’s a sunrise, a poem, or a glowing universe projected onto the side of a building, I try to make things that make people feel something real.

I’ve been creating since I was a kid. Before the camera or keyboard, there were badly staged living room plays (sorry, Mom and Dad), mock radio shows with my sister, and enough daydreaming to require my own spoiler alert system. I moved on to capturing animals in the backyard with a camcorder and learned to tell stories with whatever tools I could get my hands on.

Somewhere along the way, I added photography, writing, design, video, and light-based art to my toolbelt. The styles change. The mediums evolve. But the heart of it—seeing the world with fresh eyes—has always stayed the same.

Now, thanks to this little corner of the internet, I get to share that work with you. So—thank you for being here. I hope you find something that resonates, whether it’s a photo that brings you calm, a poem that stirs something up, or a projection that makes you stop and wonder.

If you want to see what’s new, explore the site. If you want to support the project, check out the shop. And if none of this is your thing… well, kindly lie to your friends and tell them I’m amazing.

(Disclaimer: I do not endorse lying. It’s a slippery slope that can end in serious injury and sometimes death.)

For Those Who Just Want the Gist

Short version: I make things with light, words, and images because I love stories and the people who live them. I’m here to help you see your world a little differently—sometimes quieter, sometimes brighter, always intentional.

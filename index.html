The Backstory

The Backstory

While exploring the far reaches of the internet, one of life’s big questions likely flashed across your mind: “Who is Joseph Moyer?” Then, somehow, you ended up here, on this homepage, in your relentless quest for answers. But don't stop now. This entire website is dedicated to helping you learn as much as you want—followed by the closest thing to a support group once you’re finished.

I’m a photographer, writer, and projection mapping artist who believes the world is full of small moments worth noticing—and occasionally worth laughing at. Whether I’m capturing a sunrise that made me stop in my tracks, turning a blank wall into a galaxy of moving light, or stringing words together until they become something that makes you pause, I’m here to help you find beauty, humor, and a fresh perspective in the everyday.
